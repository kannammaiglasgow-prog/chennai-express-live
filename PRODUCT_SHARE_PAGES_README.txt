CHENNAI EXPRESS PRODUCT SHARE PAGES

The product folder contains one share page for every product in products.js.
These pages provide WhatsApp and Facebook with product title, price, description,
image and direct URL preview information.

AFTER ADDING OR CHANGING PRODUCTS:
1. Make sure products.js contains the latest products.
2. Double-click GENERATE_PRODUCT_SHARE_PAGES.bat.
3. Upload the updated product folder and changed website files to GitHub.

The generator updates all current and future product share pages automatically.
